What Are SolPups?

SolPups are 10,000 generative NFTs. Each SolPup is unique with its own traits, over 200+ unique traits are used. SolPup original minters will receive residual royalties FOREVER. SolPup current holders will also receive their share of royalties. SolPup holders will also be receiving airdrops, giveaways, private-access, and more!

SolPups are the first pet as part of the Sol Pets Society. We're planning on making this our ecosystem and are in the process of creating the plans for a gaming project. SolPups will always be the rarest, highest priority collection in the ecosystem.
